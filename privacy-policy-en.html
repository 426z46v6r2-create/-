Privacy Policy

Transparency and protection of your personal information

*Draft Privacy Policy for review and approval by the firm's counsel*

Welcome to the website of Yafit Shimon Touati – Law Office (the "Website" and the "Firm" respectively). We respect the privacy of the Website's users and are committed to protecting the personal information collected about them. This policy details our data collection and use practices, in accordance with the Privacy Protection Law, 5741-1981 (the "Law").

1. Identity of the Data Controller

The data controller responsible for processing your data is:
Yafit Shimon Touati – Law Office
Email for privacy inquiries: office@yst-law.com

2. Types of Collected Information

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide voluntarily: Name, email address, phone number, and message content when you contact us via website forms or email.
  • Automated technical information: IP address, browser type, operating system, browsing times, and website usage data, collected via Cookies and analytics tools.

3. Purposes of Processing

The information is collected and processed for the following purposes:

  • Providing legal services, responding to your inquiries, and contacting you.
  • Ongoing operation, security, and improvement of the website browsing experience.
  • Compliance with legal requirements and management of legal proceedings if necessary.

4. Obligation to Provide Information (per Amendment 13)

It is clarified that you are generally under no legal obligation to provide us with any personal information. Providing the information depends on your free will and consent. However, without providing basic contact details, we will not be able to get back to you and respond to your inquiry.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing is based on your consent (upon proactive contact), for taking steps prior to entering into a contract (service provision), and/or due to the Firm's legitimate interest in managing and securing the Website.

6. Data Retention and Security

We take accepted information security measures to protect your data. Information will be retained only for the time necessary to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law (e.g., for legal documentation or accounting purposes). We may transfer information to service providers (such as hosting or mailing services) who are committed to maintaining confidentiality and privacy, including services located abroad, subject to proper oversight.

7. Your Rights as a Data Subject

In accordance with the Privacy Protection Law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Review: To request to review the information held about you by us.
  • Right to Rectification: To request to correct or delete information that is incorrect, incomplete, or unclear.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: To withdraw your consent to data processing in the future (subject to legal limitations).
  • Complaint: The right to file a complaint with the Privacy Protection Authority if your rights have been violated.

To exercise your rights, you can contact us at: office@yst-law.com

8. Cookies

The Website uses "Cookies" (small text files saved in the browser) for proper operation, security, and statistical analysis. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via "Cookie Settings" at the bottom of the page, or through your browser settings.

Categories:
• Strictly Necessary: Required for website operation.
• Functional & Analytics: To improve user experience and statistics (subject to your consent via the banner).

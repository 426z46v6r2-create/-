Accessibility Statement

Committed to digital accessibility for everyone

General

Yafit Shimon Touati Law Office is committed to making its services and website accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We operate in accordance with the Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Law, 5758-1998, and its regulations.

This statement details the accessibility efforts made on this website, which are based on the Israeli Standard IS 5568 ("Guidelines for Internet Content Accessibility") at Level AA, based on the WCAG 2.0 guidelines. We also strive to implement WCAG 2.1 guidelines wherever possible.

Website Accessibility Measures

This website has undergone various adjustments to ensure convenient and accessible browsing:

  • Structure and Headings: The site is built with a clear, hierarchical structure using standard headings (H1-H6) to facilitate orientation and screen reader usage.
  • Keyboard Navigation: The site allows full navigation using the keyboard (Tab, Shift+Tab, Enter).
  • Visual Contrast: Texts and buttons were designed with color contrast that meets standard requirements for optimal readability.
  • Contact Forms: Form fields are defined with clear labels and support assistive technologies.
  • Alternative Text (Alt Text): Meaningful images have alternative text describing their content.
  • Responsiveness: The site is adapted for browsing on mobile devices (smartphones) and tablets.
  • Text Resizing: Display size can be increased using the browser (Ctrl +) or the built-in accessibility tool.

Known Limitations

Despite our efforts to make all site pages accessible, specific parts may not yet be fully accessible or may have technological deviations (such as third-party components).

We continue our efforts to improve accessibility as an integral part of site maintenance. If you encounter any issue, we would appreciate it if you let us know.
